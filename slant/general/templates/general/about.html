Behind the Slant

My name is Andrew Milligan. I am a graduate student, a software engineer, a data enthusiast, and an engaged citizen. This project is primarily a hobby and now an accidental portfolio. I built various pieces of this site over a long period of time, but all the code behind it is available on GitHub.

I have drawn a great deal of inspiration for the project from a wide variety of sources. Some of the most prominent include the New York Times graphics team, The Marshall Project, and the Jacobin. Additionally, Junk Charts has had a strong influence on my data visualization style.
